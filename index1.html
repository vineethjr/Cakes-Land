A Sweet History of Cakes

Did you know that cakes have been around since ancient times? The first cakes were more like bread, sweetened with honey, and were often baked for special occasions. Over time, cakes evolved, with the introduction of refined sugar and baking powder in the 19th century revolutionizing how cakes were made. Today, cakes are enjoyed around the world in countless varieties, from the classic sponge cake to decadent chocolate creations. Whether celebrating a birthday, wedding, or simply indulging in a sweet treat, cakes have become a universal symbol of celebration and joy.

Funny Facts About Cakes

  • The world's largest cake weighed over 150,000 pounds – that's heavier than a blue whale!
  • The first birthday cake with candles dates back to ancient Greece, where people would light candles to represent the glow of the moon.
  • In 2011, a couple in India broke a record by creating a 27,000-pound wedding cake!
  • Did you know? The term "cakewalk" originated from a dance that was so easy, the prize was a cake!
  • The most expensive cake ever made cost $75 million – it was adorned with diamonds and precious stones.
